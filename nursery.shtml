Nursery

Our Nursery program at Widewings Preschool is designed to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment for children aged 3+ years old.

We understand the importance of the early years in a child's development and strive to create a program that lays a strong foundation for future learning. In our Nursery program, children engage in a variety of age-appropriate activities that promote social, emotional, cognitive, and physical development.

Learning at Widewings

At Widewings Preschool, we take pride in our unique approach to learning, which is centered around the belief that children learn best when they are actively engaged and interested in what they are learning. Our curriculum is designed to be hands-on and experiential, allowing children to explore, discover, and learn in a natural and meaningful way.

We believe that learning should be a joyful and enriching experience, which is why we incorporate play, creativity, and imagination into our daily activities.
